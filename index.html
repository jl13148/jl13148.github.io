Jinting Liu

Hi! I am a senior Computer Science student at New York University Shanghai. My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, systems, and networks. I aim to build efficient and reliable systems for machine learning under real world constraints.

I am currently working with Prof. Shengjie Wang on visual language models and their applications in data management systems. Previously, I worked with Prof. Guyue Liu at Peking University NetSys Lab on machine learning systems and networks. I was also an undergraduate researcher worked with Prof. Michail Maniatakos at the NYU Abu Dhabi MoMA Lab on privacy-preserving machine learning.

I am seeking a Ph.D. position starting Fall 2026 in systems for machine learning.

Research Interests

  • Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning
